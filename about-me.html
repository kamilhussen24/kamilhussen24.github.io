Kamil Hussen

Bangladeshi Music Artist

Content Creator · FinTech Developer

Kamil Hussen is a Bangladeshi music artist, content creator, and FinTech developer from Moulvibazar, Sylhet. Active in the online world since 2015, he is known for his work in Web3, crypto payment solutions, digital marketing, and video editing.

His music is available on Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon Music. Alongside his music career, Kamil builds payment systems, Telegram bots, and Web3 tools under his brand KDex — combining technology and creativity to solve real-world digital problems.

Over the years, he has worked on FinTech payment gateways, crypto trading bots, and community building — growing a 3,700+ member Telegram community focused on crypto, cards, and borderless payments. His expertise spans content creation, SEO, digital advertising, and blockchain technology.
